Lung Cancer Screening
Benefits:
  • Reduces lung cancer mortality
  • Detects cancer in early stages
  • Non-invasive procedure
Requirements:
  • Smokers aged 50-80
  • Smoking history of 20+ years
  • No active respiratory infections or medical issues
